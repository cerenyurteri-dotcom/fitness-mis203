5x5 Strength Program

Focuses on compound lifts for 5 sets of 5 reps. Ideal for building foundational strength (Beginner).

5x5 Starting Strength

  • **Monday (A):** Squat 5x5, Bench Press 5x5, Barbell Row 5x5.
  • **Wednesday (B):** Squat 5x5, Overhead Press 5x5, Deadlift 1x5.
  • **Friday (A/B):** Alternate A-B workouts. Focus on adding weight to the bar.
  • Estimated calories burned 280-450.

Push Pull Legs (PPL)

High-frequency split targeting pushing, pulling, and leg muscles on separate days for balanced growth (Advenced).

PPL Schedule

  • **Day 1 (Push):** Incline dumbell press 4x12, Shoulder Press 4x12, Tricep pushdown 3x15.
  • **Day 2 (Pull):** Weighted Pullups 3x5, Cable Row 4x10, Barbell curl 3x10.
  • **Day 3 (Legs):** Squats 3x5, Split Stance RDL 3x10, Calve raise 3x20.
  • Estimated calories burned 280-450.

Upper Lower Split

Dividing the body into upper and lower days, allowing for twice-weekly frequency (intermediate).

Upper Lower

  • **Day 1:** Upper Body (Bench Press 5x5, Barbell Row 5x5, Shoulder Press 5x5).
  • **Day 2:** Lower Body (Squat 4x10, RDL 4x10, Leg extension 3x15 ).
  • **Day 3-5:** Rest, then Upper Body (Volume) & Lower Body (Strength).
  • Estimated calories burned 280-450.

üèÉ Cardio & Conditioning Programs

HIIT Training

Alternating short, intense bursts of anaerobic exercise with less intense recovery periods. Highly effective for burning calories quickly but hard to recover from(Intermediate).

HIIT Routine

  • **Routine:** 20 seconds max effort sprint followed by 40 seconds rest.
  • **Duration:** Repeat 8-10 times.
  • **Frequency:** 3 times per week.
  • Estimated calories burned 500-800+.

LISS

Steady exercise (jogging or biking) at a comfortable pace. Good for recovery and improving endurance base (Beginner).

LISS Routine

  • **Routine:** Maintain 35-50% Max Heart Rate.
  • **Duration:** 45 - 60 minutes.
  • **Examples:** Walking, light jogging, swimming or cycling.
  • Estimated calories burned 350-500.
